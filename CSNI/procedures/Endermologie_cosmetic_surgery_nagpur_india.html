Endermologie

Endermologie was originally developed in the 1980's as a non-surgical method to treat scars in burn victims.

Endermologie has been extensively studied and approved by the FDA to specifically make the claim that it does reduce cellulite.

Endermologie is a new cellulite treatment many women are interested about since it is non invasive; meaning no surgery is performed. The technique is so gentle and effective; it is slowly becoming one of the most popular cellulite treatments today. With results as losing up to 3 inches in cellulite, it's no wonder women can't stop talking about it.

Frequently asked questions?

What is endermologie?

Endermologie was originally developed in the 1980's as a non-surgical method to treat scars in burn victims. These patients began to notice improvements in their cellulite and soon endermologie was considered by the FDA the first cellulite treatment.

Endermologie is a massage machine with two rollers that when rolled over the top layer of fat makes a fold. This folding action stretches the connective tissue that traps cellulite underneath the skin. As the skin stretches the appearance of cellulite is reduced along with inches of your cellulite and body measurements. The benefits of endermologie are increased circulation, relief of muscular pain, reduction of muscle spasms and cellulite.

Endermologie is not only for cellulite, though. It can be used for those suffering from poor blood circulation, muscular pains, and to exfoliate the skin to smoothen it. In fact, the procedure was originally invented over three decades ago in France for the purposes of acting as rehabilitative treatment for damaged muscular tissue, burns and scars. The reduction of cellulite was simply a side effect to the procedure.

How does an endermologie session go?

Endermologie is carried out by licensed endermologie therapists, who operate the vacuum-like mechanism on patients. Basically you lie down and make yourself comfortable, and the therapist does all the work, running the little gadget over your problem areas. The whole thing feels like a deep massage, and can be very relaxing.

The entire session lasts about 30-45 minutes, and the pressure can be made milder or stronger depending on your preference. The worst kind of side effects you may get from a session is some mild soreness or some tenderness in the massaged area, but these are rare. Nonetheless, if you don't tolerate pain very well, think twice before trying out endermologie treatment.

Of course, it would be impossible to see huge results after the first session. A good endermologie cellulite center may ask you to get sessions twice a week during the first month, once a week during the second month, and then once a month for every succeeding month. You'll want to maintain the benefits of endermologie by sticking to the program.

Endermologie information cvellulite treatments

Endermologie works in a series of treatments. Generally, you will have a minimum of fourteen beginning treatments. However, some people require twenty-eight beginning treatments. These are called beginning treatments because they are typically performed once or twice a week during the initial period.

After this period, if the appropriate results were attained, the patient will only need to return to the endermologist once a month for maintenance treatments. The treatments generally take about thirty minutes each time and are a total body treatment. This provides the patient with the maximum benefits of endermologie, not only in targeting cellulite but treating the body as a hole for the benefits listed above.

What is great about endermologie is that it generally only takes six treatments before you will start to see results. As the treatments continue, you will start to see dramatic improvement of cellulite or should we say the lack thereof.

Frequently asked questions?

Does endermologie reduce cellulite?

Yes. Endermologie has been extensively studied and approved by the FDA to specifically make the claim that it does reduce cellulite. However, it does not permanently remove cellulite. Results can last anywhere from 6 months to year.

Is endermologie better than liposuction?

Absolutely. Actually any cellulite cream or treatment is better than liposuction. liposuction does not reduce cellulite but only makes it worse! liposuction removes fat from the middle layer of skin, leaving the top layer of skin still dimpled and full of cellulite. Endermologie works directly on the dimpled top layer of skin, breaking up the fat trapped underneath effectively reducing the appearance of cellulite.

Is the endermologie painful?

Not at all! It's as relaxing and soothing as a deep tissue massage.

Endermologie side effects

Similar to a deep tissue massage you will experience an overall feeling of relation. You may also experience temporary bruising; which is a common result of deep tissue massage.

Frequently asked questions?

Are endermologie results permanent?

No. After endermologie treatments it's very important to stay away from factors which contribute to cellulite. If conditions are not changed, the cellulite will return in the state it was before. Doctors recommend patients exercise regularly, maintain a healthy diet and go in for 1-2 endermologie treatments per month for optimal results.

Benefits

Aside from its obvious benefit of getting rid of cellulite, endomologie also works to:

  • reduce muscle spasms
  • muscular pain relief
  • increase the circulation
  • reduce measurements of the body

As you can see, while endermologie has a direct impact on cellulite it can also be used to help in many other areas as well.

Frequently asked questions?

Is endermologie treatment safe and comfortable?

Spa massage - endermologie

Many patients feel extremely relaxed and enjoy their endermologie treatments because it feels much like a massage. It is safe because it is non-evasive. It requires no surgeries, no shots, and can be adjusted to ensure a patients comfort. Upon leaving the treatment most people tend to feel relaxed all over and find that their energy levels have a large boost.

The only real side effect from endermologie is the possibility of bruising, which can occur even when getting a massage. The bruising is temporary and very minor and will go away in a very short period of time.
